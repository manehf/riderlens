PRIVACY POLICY

Effective July 7, 2026

RiderLens is a mobile app that turns short riding clips into pose-analyzed records. This policy explains what data the app handles and where it goes. The short version: your library lives on your phone, clips are uploaded only to be processed, and we don't sell anything about you.

What we collect and where it lives

  • Your videos and records (clips, pose overlays, tags, timestamps) are stored locally on your device. There are no accounts and no cloud library in the current version.
  • Your rider profile (optional: name, photo, body measurements, units) is stored only on your device.
  • Processing uploads: when a record is created, the selected clip is uploaded to our processing server to generate the trimmed clip, pose overlay, and metrics. Uploads are kept temporarily for processing and retry, then expire and are deleted. We do not build a copy of your library on our servers.

AI processing

To find the action in your clip and label key moments, sampled frames from your upload are analyzed by an AI service (Anthropic's Claude API). Frames are sent for that analysis only; per Anthropic's API terms, API inputs are not used to train their models.

What we don't do

  • No sale or sharing of your data with advertisers.
  • No access to your photo library beyond the clips you explicitly pick.
  • No tracking across other apps or websites.

Crash reporting

The app and our processing server use Sentry to collect crash and error reports so we can fix problems. A report contains technical details (what failed, stack trace, device model, OS version, app version). Reports never include your videos, images, name, or profile data, and we configure Sentry not to collect IP-based identity. Crash data is retained by Sentry for a limited period and used only for debugging.

Analytics

Future versions may collect anonymous usage events (for example, "a record was created") to improve the app. If added, this section will be updated before release.

Sharing

When you share a clip from RiderLens, you decide where it goes. Shared clips may carry a RiderLens watermark and a link to this site.

Deleting your data

Deleting a record in the app deletes its files from your device, including the copied source video. Deleting the app removes everything. Server-side processing uploads expire automatically.

Children

RiderLens is not directed at children under 13.
