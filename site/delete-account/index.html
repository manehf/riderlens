Delete your data

Your data is yours to delete.

RiderLens has no accounts — your library lives on your device, so deletion is in your hands and takes effect immediately.

Delete a single record

Open the record and tap Delete. This removes the record's files from your device, including the copied source video. Originals you filmed with your camera app stay safely in your Photos/Gallery.

Delete everything

Uninstalling RiderLens removes the entire library, your rider profile, and all app data from the device.

What about our servers?

Clips are uploaded only while a record is being processed and expire automatically shortly afterwards — there is no server-side copy of your library to delete. Crash reports (technical data only, never footage) are retained by Sentry for a limited period.

Anything else

If you have any data question or request, email hello@riderlens.app and we'll take care of it.
