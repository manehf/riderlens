Reading the specimen: watch the HUD numerals and the metric column — that is where a technical mono separates from Inter. All four families are SIL OFL (free to bundle in the Expo app) and load via @expo-google-fonts. Numbers use font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums so 86 / 172 / 32 stay aligned in columns. Archivo stands in for Mona Sans here because Mona is not on Google Fonts.
